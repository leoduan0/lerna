# Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality

The **Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality** states that

<Align type="center">

{/* prettier-ignore */}
$$ \left(\sum*{i=1}^n a_i^2\right)\left(\sum*{i=1}^n b*i^2\right) \geq \left(\sum*{i=1}^n a_ib_i\right)^2 $$.

</Align>

The inequality can also be written in vector form:

<Align type="center">

{/* prettier-ignore */}
$$ \lVert \vec{a} \rVert \lVert \vec{b} \rVert \geq |\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}| $$.

</Align>
