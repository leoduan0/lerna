# Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality

The **Cauchy-Schwarz Inequality** (also known as the **Cauchy-Bunyakovsky-Schwarz Inequality**) states that:

$$ \left(\sum_{i=1}^n a_i^2\right)\left(\sum_{i=1}^n b_i^2\right) \geq \left(\sum_{i=1}^n a_ib_i\right)^2 $$.

The inequality can also be written in vector form:

$$ \lVert \vec{a} \rVert \lVert \vec{b} \rVert \geq |\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}| $$.
