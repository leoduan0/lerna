# Difference of Two Squares

**Difference of two wquares** refers to:

<Align type="center">

$$ a^2-b^2 = (a-b)(a+b) $$

</Align>

<Block text="Derivation">

| Procedure             | Annotation                           |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| $$ a^2-b^2 $$         |                                      |
| $$ = a^2-b^2+ab-ab $$ | Add and subtract $$ ab $$            |
| $$ = a(a+b)-b(a+b) $$ | Group and factor $$ a $$ and $$ b $$ |
| $$ = (a-b)(a+b) $$    | Factor $$ (a+b) $$                   |

</Block>
