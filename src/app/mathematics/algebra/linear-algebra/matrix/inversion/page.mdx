# Inversion

A matrix cannot be divided albeit can be multiplied by its inverse, which could be found as so:

<Align type="center">

$$ A^{-1} = \frac{\operatorname{adj}(A)}{\det(A)} $$

</Align>

Where $$ \operatorname{adj}(A) = {\operatorname{cof}(A)}^T $$ and $$ A \times A^{-1} = I $$.
