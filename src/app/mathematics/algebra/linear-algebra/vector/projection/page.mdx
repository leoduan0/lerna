# Projection

<Rule
  statement={
    <MathExpression
      math="\operatorname{proj}_{\vec{b}}\vec{a} = \frac{\vec{a} \cdot \vec{b}}{{\lVert \vec{b} \rVert}^2}\vec{b}"
      type="block"
    />
  }
  terms={[
    {
      term: "\\operatorname{proj}_{\\vec{b}}\\vec{a}",
      annotation: (
        <span>
          Projection of <MathExpression math="\vec{a}" type="inline" /> on{" "}
          <MathExpression math="\vec{b}" type="inline" />
        </span>
      ),
    },
    {
      term: "\\vec{a}",
      annotation: <span>A vector</span>,
    },
    {
      term: "\\vec{b}",
      annotation: <span>A vector</span>,
    },
  ]}
/>

<Block text="Example">

If $$ \vec{a} = \langle 14, 22, 31 \rangle $$ and $$ \vec{b} = \langle 4, -2, 5 \rangle $$, then $$ \operatorname{proj}_{\vec{b}}\vec{a} = \frac{\langle 14, 22, 31 \rangle \cdot \langle 4, -2, 5 \rangle}{{\lVert \langle 4, -2, 5 \rangle \rVert}^2} \langle 4, -2, 5 \rangle = \frac{167}{45} \langle 4, -2, 5 \rangle = \left\langle \frac{668}{45}, -\frac{334}{45}, \frac{167}{9} \right\rangle $$.

</Block>

<Block text="Example">

If $$ \vec{a} = \langle 1, 2, 3 \rangle $$ and $$ \vec{b} = \langle 4, 5, 6 \rangle $$, then $$ \operatorname{proj}_{\vec{b}}\vec{a} = \frac{\langle 1, 2, 3 \rangle \cdot \langle 4, 5, 6 \rangle}{||\langle 4, 5, 6 \rangle||^2}\langle 4, 5, 6 \rangle = \frac{32}{77} \langle 4, 5, 6 \rangle = \left\langle \frac{128}{77}, \frac{160}{77}, \frac{192}{77} \right\rangle $$.

</Block>
