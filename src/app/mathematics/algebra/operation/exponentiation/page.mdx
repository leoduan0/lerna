# Exponentiation

<Rule
  statement={<Math math="a^n = \prod_{i=1}^n a" type="block" />}

## Rules

- $$ a^0 = 1 $$
- $$ a^m \cdot a^n = a^{m+n} $$
- $$ \frac{a^m}{a^n} = a^{m-n} $$
- $$ (a^m)^n = a^{m \cdot n} $$
- $$ a^{-m} = \frac{1}{a^m} $$
