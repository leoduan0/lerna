# Sophie Germain's Inequality

**Sophie Germain's inequality** states that:

<Align type="center">

$$ a^4+4b^4 = (a^2+2ab+2b^2)(a^2-2ab+2b^2) = ((a+b)^2+b^2)((a-b)^2+b^2) $$

</Align>

<Block text={"Derivation"}>

| Procedure                                | Annotation                                                                                                |
| ---------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| $$ a^4+4b^4 $$                           |                                                                                                           |
| $$ = (a^2)^2+(2b^2)^2 $$                 | Rewrite                                                                                                   |
| $$ = (a^2)^2+(2b^2)^2+(2ab)^2-(2ab)^2 $$ | Add and subtract $$ (2ab)^2 $$                                                                            |
| $$ = (a^2+2b^2)^2+(2b^2)^2-(2ab)^2 $$    | $$ (a^2)^2+(2ab)^2+(2b^2)^2 = (a^2+2b^2)^2 $$                                                             |
| $$ = (a^2+2b^2+2ab)(a^2+2b^2-2ab) $$     | Use the difference of two squares                                                                         |
| $$ = ((a+b)^2+b^2)((a-b)^2+b^2) $$       | $$ a^2+2b^2+2ab = a^2+2ab+b^2+b^2 = (a+b)^2+b^2 $$ and $$ a^2+2b^2-2ab = a^2-2ab+b^2+b^2 = (a-b)^2+b^2 $$ |

</Block>
