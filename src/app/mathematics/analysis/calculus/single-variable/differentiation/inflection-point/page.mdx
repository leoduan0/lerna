# Inflection Point

For all points where $$ f^{\prime \prime}(k) = 0 $$ or is undefined, if the signs of the second derivative just to the left of $$ f^{\prime \prime}(k) $$ compared to the right are different, then $$ k $$ is a **point of inflection**.
