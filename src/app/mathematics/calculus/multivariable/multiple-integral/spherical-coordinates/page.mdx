Multiple integrals in **spherical coordinates** requires an understanding of the diagram below.

From the diagram above, we can convert spherical coordinates to cartesian:

- $$ x=\rho\sin(\phi)\cos(\theta) $$

- $$ y=\rho\sin(\phi)\sin(\theta) $$

- $$ z=\rho\cos(\phi) $$

Now, we can apply multiple integrals to spherical coordinates just like we do to cartesian.
