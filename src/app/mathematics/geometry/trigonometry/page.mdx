# Trigonometry

## Radians and Degrees

There are $$ 360\degree $$ (360 **degrees**) and $$ 2\pi $$ (2π **radians**) in a circle.

## Trigonometric Functions

Given a unit circle, for some point on the circumference of that circle, we draw a triangle with the hypotenuse from the point to the origin, the height from the point vertically to the x-axis, and the width from the point horizontally the y-axis. From so we obtain the following **trigonometric functions**, where $$ \theta $$ is the angle between the width and the hypotenuse:

| Function  | Notation           | Definition                      | Period     | Domain                                                                                                       | Range                                | Symmetry | Amplitude |
| --------- | ------------------ | ------------------------------- | ---------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------ | -------- | --------- |
| Sine      | $$ \sin(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{height}{hypotenuse} $$ | $$ 2\pi $$ | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$                                                                                         | $$ [-1, 1] $$                        | Odd      | 1         |
| Cosine    | $$ \cos(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{width}{hypotenuse} $$  | $$ 2\pi $$ | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$                                                                                         | $$ [-1, 1] $$                        | Even     | 1         |
| Tangent   | $$ \tan(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{height}{width} $$      | $$ \pi $$  | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$ except $$ \frac{\pi}{2} + n \pi $$ for all values of $$ n $$ where $$ \{ n \in \Z \} $$ | $$ (-\infty, \infty) $$              | Odd      | N/A       |
| Cosecant  | $$ \csc(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{hypotenuse}{height} $$ | $$ 2\pi $$ | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$ except $$ n \pi $$ for all values of $$ n $$ where $$ \{ n \in \Z \} $$                 | $$ (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) $$ | Odd      | 1         |
| Secant    | $$ \sec(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{hypotenuse}{width} $$  | $$ 2\pi $$ | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$ except $$ \frac{\pi}{2} + n \pi $$ for all values of $$ n $$ where $$ \{ n \in \Z \} $$ | $$ (-\infty, -1] \cup [1, \infty) $$ | Even     | 1         |
| Cotangent | $$ \cot(\theta) $$ | $$ \frac{width}{height} $$      | $$ \pi $$  | $$ \{ x \in \R \} $$ except $$ n \pi $$ for all values of $$ n $$ where $$ \{ n \in \Z \} $$                 | $$ (-\infty, \infty) $$              | Odd      | N/A       |
