# Notes and Rests

**Notes** in musical notation either denotes pitch or differentiate different constituent instruments (the latter is more common among percussion instruments).

**Rests** in musical notation denotes a period of silence.

There are many different types of notes and rests for different sounds, periods of silence, and lengths.

## Staffs

Notes are notated on a musical staff, and staffs consist of five lines.

Notes can go on top of a line or between two adjacent lines.

The higher the note is on a staff, the higher the note is in pitch.
