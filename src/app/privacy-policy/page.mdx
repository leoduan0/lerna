# Privacy Policy

This page outlines Lerna's ("we", "our", "us") behaviour with user data.

## Information We Collect

- We do not collect any information from users.
- We do not have any databases to store any dynamically generated information.
- This project is deployed on [Vercel](https://vercel.com) and is subject to their [Terms of Service](https://vercel.com/legal/terms) and [Privacy Policy](https://vercel.com/legal/privacy-policy).
